About The Fasciculus Project

Description

The Fasciculus Project will provide several C# packages for public use.
For now, it is a one-man-show.

Name and Logo

A wooden rod or slab can be broken easily.

If you bind several of them together, you get a "bundle" (Latin: "fascis").
A bundle is stronger than a single rod.
That's the logo.

The Latin word for a small bundle is "fasciculus".
Hence the name.

More Trivia

A fascis is a symbol for the strength of many.

If you bind several rods together around the handle of an axe such that the axe's head sticks out on top of it, you get a "fasces".
Such a fasces was the symbol of Roman authorities.

The political word "fascism" is derived from "fascis".
The Fasciculus Project isn't political.
